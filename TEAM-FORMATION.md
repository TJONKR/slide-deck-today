How Teams Will Form
Matchmaking starts immediately after the opening remarks.
Anyone looking for a team or co-founders will pitch for 30 seconds. Hard stop.
Pitch your:
Idea (if you have one)
Skills
What you are looking for
After the pitches, we let teams form organically. We can’t guarantee you can find a team mate, but you can always participate!
Organizers will facilitate, not assign.

No long debates. No overthinking. Move fast.

Team Size
Recommended: 2–3 people
Solo is allowed.
Small teams move faster. Speed wins hackathons.

Come Early
Arrive early to start meeting people before the official matchmaking.
The best teams usually form before the session even starts.
If you wait until the last minute, expect fewer options.
