  2. THIS IS TAG                                                                                                                                                                              
  - A community of builders, hackers, and creators in Amsterdam
  - Think, build, ship, repeat                                                                                                                                                                
  - For the obsessed, creative and talented people who create. Every single day.
  - We're all ambitious, all excited, all open to explore anything about building with AI
  - The permanent hackathon - the energy of a hackathon, but every single day                                                                                                                 
   
  3. THIS BUILDING / BIGGER THAN US                                                                                                                                                           
  - This isn't just about TAG                                  
  - Look around you - this whole building, this whole area                                                                                                                                    
  - Norrsken (Swedish impact fund) is moving in around the corner
  - TechLeap wants to join                                                                                                                                                                    
  - AI.AM is already part of this ecosystem                    
  - And we're right here. TAG. To Achieve Greatness.
  - This could become something bigger than any of us                                                                                                                                         
   
  4. EUROPE'S PROBLEM                                                                                                                                                                         
  - We have a fragmentation problem. Europe, the Netherlands - we're scattered.
  - One branch isn't strong. But a few branches together? Unbreakable.                                                                                                                        
  - We're too close-minded. We gotta push past that.                  
  - Europe has the talent. What we're missing is the irrational belief that we deserve to win.                                                                                                
  - How do you fix that? By coming together. By showing what you build.                                                                                                                       
  - A hackathon is the perfect gesture for that.                       
                                                                                                                                                                                              
  5. SHOW UP MORE                                                                                                                                                                             
  - I'm so happy everyone is here - because this is how we solve that problem
  - But we gotta do this more often                                                                                                                                                           
  - This space is the permanent hackathon. It's open.          
  - We all love hybrid, love working from home - but to get to that next step you gotta be in the same room                                                                                   
  - Come here more often. Meet your people. Get inspired by how others build.                              
  - This is that place.
